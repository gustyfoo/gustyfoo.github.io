Wildlife Management


 

Black bears populations are rapidly expanding in California resulting in increasing reports of human–wildlife conflicts. Working in close collaboration with government personnel, I use molecular mark-recapture techniques and spatially-explicit capture-recapture models to quantify black bear abundances and densities.
